1. No mind to think.
	没有可以思考的心智。
	
	No will to break.
	没有可以屈从的意志。
	
	No voice to cry suffering.
	没有为苦难哭泣的声音。
	
	Born of God and Void.
	生于神与虚空之手。
	
	You shall seal the blinding light that plagues their dreams.
	你必封印在众人梦中散布瘟疫的障目之光。
	
	You are the Vessel.
	你是容器。
	
	You are the Hollow Knight.
	你是空洞骑士。


2. All tragedy erased. I see only wonders.
	我忘记了所有悲剧，看到的都是奇迹。


3. In wilds beyond they speak your name with reverence and regret,
	在远方的荒野里，提到你的名字它们都饱含敬畏与惋惜之情，
	
	For none could tame our savage souls yet you the challenge met,
	没有谁能驯服我们野蛮的灵魂，而你接受了挑战，
	
	Under palest watch, you taught, you changed. Base instincts were redeemed,
	在最苍白的注视下，你教授，我们改变，本能得到了救赎，
	
	A world you gave to bug and beast as they had never dreamed.
	你为虫子和野兽带来了它们做梦也想不到的世界。


4. To witness secrets sealed, one must endure the harshest punishment.
	想要见证尘封的秘密，就要承受最严厉的惩罚。


5. In the Black Vault far above. Through its sacrifice Hallownest lasts eternal.
	在那高远的黑色穹顶下，他的牺牲使圣巢永世不衰。
